# Digital-Literacy

## Project Overview

This project focuses on building digital literacy skills among students. It covers awareness about digital tools, safe internet practices, professional communication, and cybercrime prevention. As a Digital Ambassador, this project helps in guiding students to use digital platforms responsibly and effectively.

---

## Tasks Completed

### Task 1 – Infographic

Created a digital literacy infographic using Canva. The infographic explains digital literacy, useful tools, safe internet practices, and email etiquette.

---

### Task 2 – Digital Portfolio

Created professional profiles on GitHub and LinkedIn. Updated details such as name, education, and learning goals.

---

### Task 3 – Platforms

Solved a beginner-level coding problem on HackerRank and created a Google Form quiz for digital literacy awareness.

---

### Task 4 – Email Etiquette

Drafted two professional emails and created a Do’s and Don’ts checklist for responsible social media use.

---

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

Prepared a case study on UPI fraud and created a prevention checklist with safety tips and reporting methods.
